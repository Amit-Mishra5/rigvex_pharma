Why Our Formulas Cost More to Make

We choose better. Every single time.

Most brands use the cheapest form of every ingredient. We don't. Here's what that difference means for your body.

Ferrous Sulphate
→
Ferrous Bisglycinate

Bisglycinate is a chelated form of iron — gentler on the stomach, significantly better absorbed. No constipation. No nausea. Just iron that actually reaches your cells.

Folic Acid
→
L-Methylfolate

Up to 40% of people cannot properly convert folic acid due to MTHFR gene variants. L-Methylfolate is the active, ready-to-use form — critical during pregnancy, works for everyone.

Vitamin K2 MK-4
→
Vitamin K2 MK-7

MK-7 has a dramatically longer half-life than MK-4 — one daily dose effectively directs calcium to your bones, not your arteries. The research is unambiguous.
