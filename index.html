Rigvex Pharma is a women's health-focused nutraceutical company, developing science-backed formulations for hormonal balance, prenatal care, and postpartum recovery.

Our team is guided by a simple principle: science should serve people first. From sourcing raw materials to final quality checks, every step is built around consistency, compliance, and trust.

We are committed to the highest standards of quality and safety. Our products are manufactured in FSSAI-licensed facilities and follow WHO-GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) guidelines, with formulations developed in line with ISO 22000 food safety management standards.

Every batch undergoes strict quality checks aligned with HACCP (Hazard Analysis & Critical Control Points) protocols, ensuring consistency, purity, and safety from raw material sourcing to the final product on your shelf.
