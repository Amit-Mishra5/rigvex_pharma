Why Formulation Quality Matters

Most brands choose cheaper. We never do.

The form of an ingredient determines how much your body can actually use. We always use the superior bioavailable form — at the right dose.

Ferrous Sulphate
Ferrous Bisglycinate

Chelated bisglycinate absorbs significantly better with minimal GI side effects. No constipation, no nausea — just iron that actually reaches your cells.

Folic Acid
L-Methylfolate

Up to 40% of people have MTHFR variants that prevent folic acid conversion. L-Methylfolate is the ready-to-use active form — it bypasses that entirely.

Cyanocobalamin
Methylcobalamin

Methylcobalamin is the neurologically active form of B12 — it crosses the blood-brain barrier and is retained longer in tissues. Critical for neuropathy support.

Vitamin K2 MK-4
Vitamin K2 MK-7

MK-7 has a dramatically longer biological half-life than MK-4 — one daily dose effectively directs calcium into bones, not arteries. The research is unambiguous.
